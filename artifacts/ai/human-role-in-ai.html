Three Core Pillars

What remains uniquely human?

AI expands the option space. Humans compress it into direction, priority, and quality.

01

Vibe & taste

审美品味与领域直觉

Humans decide what feels elegant, meaningful, natural, robust, and aligned with the product soul. AI can generate variations, but taste chooses the right direction.

  • What feels beautiful?
  • What feels scientifically meaningful?
  • What experience feels natural?
02

Knowledge orchestration

高层知识编排能力

Humans become conductors of engineering, design, product, business, psychology, market timing, AI tools, and team execution.

  • Connect multiple layers
  • Arrange the whole system
  • Turn local answers into global direction
03

Judgment & tradeoffs

判断、取舍与决策

Real decisions are rarely purely right or wrong. They require values, context, responsibility, and conscious choice.

  • Speed vs quality
  • Automation vs human control
  • Short-term gain vs long-term brand

High-level knowledge orchestration

The human role is not to manually play every instrument, but to define the composition: what matters, what connects, what should be amplified, and what should be removed.

人类的角色不是亲手演奏每一种乐器，而是定义整首作品：什么重要、什么相互连接、什么应被放大、什么应该被删掉。

Core Idea

Valuable humans become taste-makers, system thinkers, decision-makers, and meaning-setters.

In the AI era, the question is not only “Can AI do this task?” The better question is: “What should exist, why should it exist, and what quality standard makes it worth existing?”

在 AI 时代，关键问题不只是“AI 能不能完成这个任务？”更重要的问题是： “什么东西值得被创造？为什么值得？怎样的质量标准才配得上存在？”
